# FV TrackFlow 🌊

A minimalist, keyboard-focused workflow script for managing REAPER Track Templates.

Designed to enhance your existing workflow, FV TrackFlow provides a fast, tag-supported, and visually clean modern interface to access your track templates quickly. It streamlines your session setup with intelligent routing and an organized library system.

✨ **Key Features**

* **Smart & Responsive Interface:** A highly optimized, low-CPU modern UI that blends perfectly with any REAPER theme. It is completely resizable—dock it vertically on the side or use it as a compact square next to your other scripts.
* **Lightning-Fast Search:** Instantly find what you need by searching through track names, folder paths, or your custom tags without digging through endless native menus.
* **Intuitive Drag & Drop:** Effortlessly drag and drop track templates from the interface directly into your REAPER arrange view.
* **Adaptive & Clean Design:** The interface dynamically adapts to your actions. Buttons and options appear only when you actually need them, keeping your workspace clutter-free and your focus on the music.
* **Intelligent Routing:** Automatically moves inserted templates into their designated "Target Folders" and configures FX Sends upon insertion.
* **Database Editor:** A dedicated manager to easily organize your template library, assign atomic tags, and set up advanced routing rules.
